About Me

My name is JJ Wise, I am a senior at Jackson High School in Everett, Washington. I am also in my second year at Sno-Isle Technical Skill Center. Sometimes events happen that make a lasting impact on your life, last spring I experienced оne of those events. I was involved in a serious automobile accident. Recovery has taken months. But that time has given me time to think. I realized how quickly life could change. I have decided not to take my future for granted. I am determined to have a career that I enjoy and use all my skills and abilities, оne that provides room for professional and personal growth.

Ever since elementary I've wanted to be a game designer. It seemed fun and I'd be able to do what I love . When I was in 5th grade I joined the robotics club at school. That was my first exposure to coding and I loved it. The ability to turn code to action was fascinating. After that I forgot about coding until my junior year in high school. I signed up for Video Game design at Sno isle and from there I started to perfect my skills in coding and teamwork.

In Sno isle I learned how to be a team leader and manage my time and my team. I work really well with others and I make sure that everyone is heard. I'm really good at adapting to stressful situations and I'm able to learn really fast. Getting the things I need done on time is a number one priority because I never want to let my team down.
